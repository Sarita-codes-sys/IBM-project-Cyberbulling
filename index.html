What is Cyberbullying?

Cyberbullying is the use of digital technologies to harass, threaten, embarrass, or target another person.

Online Safety Tips

  • 🔐 Use strong passwords.
  • 🚫 Do not share personal information online.
  • 🛡️ Block and report abusive users.
  • 💭 Think before posting.
  • 🤝 Talk to a trusted adult if you feel unsafe.

What To Do If You Are Being Cyberbullied

  1. Do not respond angrily.
  2. Take screenshots as evidence.
  3. Block the bully.
  4. Report the behavior.
  5. Seek help from trusted adults.
